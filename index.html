After clicking the button below, a "portrait mode" video from your camera should start. This works on Chrome and Desktop Safari, but fails on Mobile Safari, where the aspectRatio request is ignored.

According to the way that the API for getUserMedia is written, it seems that the returned Promise should be rejected when the requirements cannot be fulfilled. However, this doesn't happen; it just silently refuses to honor the request.
